Ahmed - Keys

Ahmed rose to fame at a young age, being considered one of Saudi Arabia's premier child prodigy pianists. When he heard the intense rock music created by High Eight, his face was rocked so much that he moved to America with the sole purpose of joining the band. Although the band didn't even have a keyboardist at the time, Ahmed's amazing knack for electronic awesomeness persuaded the other members to augment their style to accommodate the new sound. Since Ahmed's contributions, the success of the band has seen an increase in popularity both in America and overseas.
